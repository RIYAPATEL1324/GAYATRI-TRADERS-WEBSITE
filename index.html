Who We Are

Gujarat's Most Trusted Material Partner

Founded in 2001 by Pulkit Patel in Ahmedabad, Gayatri Traders has spent over 24 years earning the trust of contractors, architects, and developers across Gujarat. We combine competitive pricing with an unwavering commitment to quality and timely delivery.

🏆
Authorised UltraTech Cement Agency

Gayatri Traders is an official authorised dealer of UltraTech Cement — India's largest cement brand. Get genuine product, manufacturer pricing, and assured quality directly from an authorised source.

✦
BIS-Certified Sourcing

Every product carries valid BIS certification and full manufacturer traceability. We never compromise on grade compliance — your structure's integrity depends on it.

Same-Day Dispatch

Orders confirmed before 2 PM are dispatched the same day with our fleet of 20+ GPS-tracked vehicles, covering Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Surat, and beyond.

Flexible Credit Terms

Extended payment cycles for registered contractors and bulk buyers, with complete GST documentation and mill test certificates on every order.
